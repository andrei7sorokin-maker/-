We are not building a “hotel in nature”. We are building a stay where mornings begin with birds and water, afternoons invite long walks in the woods, and evenings end with a grill, sauna, or fireplace. You can come as a couple for a quiet weekend, with family for a holiday, or with friends for a larger cabin stay.

Every location is selected so that pine forest, shoreline, water, and privacy are part of the experience. The cabins are spread across Estonia, so you can choose a short drive from Tallinn, a trip to the south, or a stay near the island coastline.
